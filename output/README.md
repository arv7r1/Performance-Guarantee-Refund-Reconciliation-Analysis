# Output Artifacts

This folder contains finalized, stakeholder-ready outputs derived from the performance guarantee analysis.

## Contents
- **refund_exposure_by_site.png**  
  Visualizes total refund exposure per site, highlighting installations with the highest financial risk.

- **breach_frequency_by_site.png**  
  Shows how often each site breached its energy guarantee over the evaluation period.

- **refund_exposure_by_region.png**  
  Aggregates refund exposure at the regional level to identify geographic concentration of risk.

## Purpose
These outputs provide a quick, visual summary of findings without requiring access to the full Excel workbook. They are intended for operational review, executive reporting, and decision support.

All charts are derived directly from the portfolio-level PivotTables.
